  • CHOCOLATE MOUSSE

    08-03-2020

    YOU

    INGREDIENTS

    3 eggs (~55g/2 oz each)

    125 g / 4.5 oz dark chocolate , bittersweet / 70% cocoa

    10 g / 0.3 oz / 2 tsp unsalted butter

    125 ml / 1/2 cup cream , full fat

    35 g / 3 tbsp caster sugar (superfine white sugar)

    DECORATIONS

    More whipped cream(covering)

    Chocolate shavings(topping)

    STEP 1

    Melt chocolate with a bit of butter

    STEP 2

    Beat egg whites and add sugar. Beat whites until firm peaks form.

    STEP 3

    Beat cream until stiff peaks form.

    STEP 4

    Pour chocolate mixture into egg whites. Fold through until incorporated and no more white lumps remain - aim for 12 folds max.

    STEP 5

    Divide mixture between 4 small glasses or pots. Refrigerate for at least 5 hours, preferably overnight.

    STEP 6

    To serve, garnish with cream and chocolate shavings. Raspberries and a tiny sprig of mint for color would also be lovely!
